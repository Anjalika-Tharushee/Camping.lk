Rent or buy

Flexible offers for different customer needs.

One page can support both rental and sales. That helps customers choose the option that suits their budget, frequency of use, and trip length.

Rent for the weekend.

Perfect for one-off camping trips, family outings, and special events.

  • Short-term use: book only for the days you need.
  • Lower upfront cost: a good option for occasional campers.
  • Simple bundles: pair tents, seating, cooking, and lights.

Buy for repeat adventures.

Best for regular campers, families, and people building their own kit.

  • Own your setup: keep the items you use most.
  • Value over time: ideal for frequent trips and outings.
  • Personalized advice: help customers choose the right gear.
